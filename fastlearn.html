Fast Learn


Writing and typing App




Fast Learn App is a platform to practice your typing and writing to become skillful and improve your speed. The Fast Learn writing and typing app is a system for improving writing and typing with a text responsive connection system and user input.
The app consists of challenges for a user that can select to type or write by hand. This is great for kids or anyone who wants to brush up on their writing and typing skills. Use your stylus pen/pencil or your computer mouse.

About The Product



The Fast Learn writing and typing app (patent-pending) is a platform to practice your typing and writing to become skillful and improve your speed and requires the user to physically interact by typing and/or handwriting. You can use a stylus pen/pencil, your finger or your computer mouse. Challenges can include numbers, letters and words at levels to reach.

When you view a number, letter or word of a category, type or write it depending on the style you choose; If you choose type on, you are typing, if you change style to handwrite on, you are now handwriting. Practice makes perfect.
Throughout years great emphasis has been placed on improving typing or writing skills, but has not added both typing and writing on the same platform as with Fast Learn writing and typing app.
